"Immortal" Jellyfish

This is Turritopsis Nutricula, the only immortal being on the planet.

  • Scientific name: Turritopsis dohrnii
  • Average length: 1mm - 4.5mm
  • Average lifespan: Immortal
  • Habitat: Caribbean (nutricula) & Mediterranean (dohrnii) Sea

Turritopsis prefer warmer waters, although they have been spotted in colder areas as well. They are tiny and their diet consists of plankton, fish eggs and small molluscs. Transdifferentiation could help scientists find new ways to repair or regenerate damaged tissue.

Call them: Medusa, Jellyfish, Jelies, Sea nettles, or whatever you want.
